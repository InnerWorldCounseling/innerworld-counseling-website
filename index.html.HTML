About

Support for Your Mental Health Journey

Hello! I’m a trauma therapist who creates a safe, supportive, and nonjudgmental space where your story can unfold at your pace. I believe healing begins when we feel truly seen and heard. My approach is person-centered, meaning our work is guided by your goals and what feels meaningful to you. I also support clients navigating anxiety and substance use—challenges that often stem from deeper pain. Whether you're facing chronic worry or using substances to cope, I’ll help you build insight, manage stress, and reconnect with your sense of peace.

I draw from a variety of evidence-based approaches—always tailored to fit you. Whether you're coping with the impact of childhood trauma, navigating the aftermath of a difficult life experience, or feeling stuck in patterns that no longer serve you, I’ll help you explore the underlying roots of your struggles—not just manage symptoms.

Starting therapy takes courage. I’m here to meet you where you are—and to walk with you every step of the way. It’s a true honor to witness your story and be a part of your healing journey.
Deirdre Kuvaas, MS, LPCC, LADC
Trauma Therapist

Qualifications and Experience

I have over 10 years of field experience working with clients struggling with mental health disorders and substance use disorders. I started my career as a drug and alcohol counselor and later pursued my journey to become a mental health counselor, earning my Masters of Science in Counseling from the University of Mary in 2017. I’ve been in practice for 8 years and hold licenses in Minnesota (LPCC #3568), North Dakota (LPCC #1330-10-15-23-548), and Ohio (LPCC #E.2404393). I am a very person-centered therapist, focusing on the root causes of mental health challenges and using methods that best fit each client, whether that’s challenging thoughts, focusing on emotions, or utilizing EMDR.
